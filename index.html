History

Founded in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR, renamed Manchester United in 1902. The club has a rich history of domestic and international trophies.

  • 1902: Renamed Manchester United
  • 1958: Munich air disaster
  • 1968: First European Cup
  • 1999: Treble – Premier League, FA Cup, Champions League
